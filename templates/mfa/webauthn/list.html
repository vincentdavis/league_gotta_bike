About Passkeys

Passkeys provide a secure, passwordless way to sign in using your device's biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello) or a hardware security key.

  • More secure than passwords - resistant to phishing attacks
  • Faster sign in - no need to type passwords or codes
  • Works across devices when synced through your browser or device ecosystem
  • Can be used as a primary authentication method or as MFA
